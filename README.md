Discord Mod Terms of Service (TOS)
Welcome to the moderation team of our Discord server! As a moderator, you are entrusted with upholding the community standards, ensuring a positive environment for all members. By accepting the position of moderator, you agree to abide by the following terms and conditions:

1. Respect and Professionalism
Treat all members, fellow moderators, and staff with respect and professionalism.
Do not engage in personal attacks, harassment, or discrimination.
Always maintain a calm and neutral tone in conflict resolution.
2. Adherence to Server Rules
Enforce the server’s community guidelines, which include no spamming, hate speech, trolling, or abusive behavior.
Follow the server’s guidelines for banning, muting, or warning members. Always apply moderation actions fairly and consistently.
3. Confidentiality
Do not share sensitive information about the server or its members outside of the moderation team without permission.
Keep any personal or private information shared by members within the community confidential.
4. Fairness in Moderation
Apply all rules consistently without favoritism or bias.
Make moderation decisions based on the server’s rules and not personal preferences or emotions.
5. Use of Moderation Tools
Only use moderation tools (such as banning, muting, and kicking) when necessary and according to the severity of the situation.
Avoid abusing your privileges for personal gain or to harm the community.
6. Reporting and Documentation
Document any significant moderation actions (such as bans or warnings) for transparency and to provide a record for future reference.
Report any server-related issues or concerns to higher-level staff promptly.
7. No Power Abuse
Moderators are expected to act in the best interests of the community. Misuse of power will not be tolerated.
Do not use your moderation position for personal advantage, such as self-promotion or preferential treatment.
8. Collaboration with Staff
Work closely with other moderators and staff members to ensure smooth community management.
Be open to feedback and constructive criticism from other team members.
9. Availability
As a moderator, you should be reasonably available to monitor the server and assist members. If you are unable to fulfill your duties due to personal reasons, notify higher-level staff.
10. Termination
Failure to adhere to these Terms of Service may result in warnings or removal from the moderation team.
Any moderator may be removed from their position at the discretion of higher-level staff or server administrators for misconduct or failure to perform duties.
